# User Guide

Welcome to the **CU Access Audit** portal! This system is designed to streamline the accessibility auditing process for physical and digital infrastructure across the Chandigarh University campus.

## Role-Based Access
The platform features customized views depending on your role.

### Admin
- **Dashboard**: High-level overview of campus accessibility health, total remediation costs, and community engagement.
- **Roadmap**: Kanban board to track accessibility improvements (To Do, In Progress, Complete).
- **Buildings & Audits**: Full access to view, edit, and export audits.
- **Reports**: Generate and download detailed statistical reports on campus accessibility.
- **Settings**: System configurations.

### Auditor
- **Dashboard**: Overview of your assigned audits and average accessibility scores.
- **Buildings**: View building records.
- **Audits**: Start new audits (utilizing the WCAG 2.1 AA checklist or physical guidelines) and submit scorecards.

### Maintenance
- **Dashboard**: Summary of open student reports and pending remediation tasks.
- **Roadmap**: Update the status of physical repair tasks (e.g., fixing ramps, installing braille signs).
- **Issues**: Address user-reported accessibility barriers.

### Student
- **Dashboard**: Track your submitted reports and campus accessibility levels.
- **Community**: Access awareness campaigns, download the tactile campus map, or participate in feedback sessions.
- **Issues**: Report physical or digital accessibility barriers directly to maintenance and administration.

## Key Features

- **Issue Reporting**: Students and staff can report barriers, complete with location data and categorization.
- **Kanban Roadmap**: Visual task management for remediation efforts.
- **Auditing Tool**: Standardized scorecards referencing RPWD Act 2016 and WCAG 2.1 compliance.
- **Analytics Reports**: Comprehensive graphs to track inclusivity improvements.
